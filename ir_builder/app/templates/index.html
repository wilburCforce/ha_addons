Broadlink IR Automation Helper

1. Select Your Broadlink Device

Choose the Broadlink RM4 device you want to use for learning a new IR command.

2. Learn a New Command

Click the button below to put your selected device into learning mode. Then, press a button on your physical remote.

3. Generate Automation YAML

Provide a name for the new command (e.g., "tv_power_on") and generate the YAML for your automation.
